Simple Interest Calculator

This simple interest calculator helps students and beginners calculate interest on a principal amount quickly and accurately. It is useful for basic finance learning, school problems, and simple loan interest calculations.

Simple interest is calculated only on the original principal amount and does not include interest on previously earned interest.

How to Calculate Simple Interest

The formula used to calculate simple interest is:

Simple Interest = (Principal × Rate × Time) / 100

This calculator uses the same formula to instantly calculate the interest amount based on the values you enter.

Example of Simple Interest Calculation

If the principal amount is ₹10,000, the rate of interest is 5% per year, and the time period is 2 years, then:

Simple Interest = (10,000 × 5 × 2) / 100 = ₹1,000

Who Should Use This Calculator

  • Students learning basic interest calculations
  • People calculating simple loan interest
  • Anyone needing quick and easy interest calculations

Difference Between Simple Interest and Compound Interest

Simple interest is calculated only on the principal amount, while compound interest is calculated on both the principal and the accumulated interest.
